As for refrigeration, MSI has opted for its technology <strong>
Silent Storm Cooling 4 </strong>

Needless to say that the processor has its own <a href="https://www.xataka.com/componentes/refrigeracion-liquida-que-como-funciona-cuando-merece-pena-apostar-ella-para-propulsar-nuestro-pc">

<p>
As for components, the tower does not fall short. In its highest configuration, the MEG AEGIS TI5 mounts a processor <a href="https://www.xataka.com/analisis/intel-core-i9-10900k-core-i5-10600k-analisis-caracteristicas-precio-especificaciones">
Intel Core I9-10900K </a>
, an Intel Z490 plate, a <strong>
MSI GeForce RTX 3080 and up to 128 GB RAM DDR4 </strong>
. It has three bays M.2 (2 x SATA / PCIE and a PCIe), two 2.5-inch bays and a 2.5-inch and a modular psu of 750W category 80 plus gold. Connectivity is in charge of WiFi 6, LAN 2.5 G + 1G and Bluetooth 5.1. </p>

<!-- BREAK 5 -->

<p>
Finally, it should be noted that the PC rides the <strong> technology
MSI Sound Tune </strong>
, which uses artificial intelligence to filter the noise of the environment and the microphone of the rest of the players to offer a clearer communication. It is a kind of noise cancellation based on a deep neuronal network that has been trained with more than 500 million data. </p>
